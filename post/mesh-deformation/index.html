Mesh Deformation

2020-03-20

A small demo of As-Rigid-As-Possible (ARAP) deformation, Click load demo to run.

Close

Controls:
Left click drag, right click rotate, scroll wheel to zoom.

step 1:
Click the Select button to choose an area to be deformed. Selected area will be marked with black dots.
By default the whole mesh is selected except for the largest boundary.
step 2:
Select control points by holding the middle button, which is highlighted with a blue marker.
Add/remove/update them shown in the list. Marked with green points.
step 3:
Drag the active control points by holding crtl. Only the checked anchors would be dragged
